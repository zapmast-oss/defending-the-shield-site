Great Week for Pittsburgh's Sun-woo Lee
Monday, April 22nd , 2002
Not every ball that came off Sun-woo Lee's bat last week was a line drive or a towering shot -- it just seemed that way to the teams that played the Pittsburgh Steelers. For his exploits, Lee was named this week's American Conference Player of the Week award.

The Pittsburgh second baseman put up 13 hits in 27 at-bats and 2 home runs while hitting .481 with 5 RBIs and 8 runs scored.

This season, Lee has hit 5 home runs and posted a .405 batting average.
