Monday, April 29th , 2002
Some bad news for the Buffalo Bills: Rafael Gomez will be out of action for about 8 months with shoulder inflammation. Reached for comment, Gomez explained that the injury occurred while throwing a shoe out his window to stop a cat from meowing. "Just my luck," he said.

For the season, Gomez is 0-and-0 with no saves and a 1.00 ERA. He has given up 9 hits in 9 innings with 6 strikeouts.
