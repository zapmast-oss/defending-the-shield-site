Miguel Lopez Shows Up to Camp...Different
Monday, February 25th , 2002
Lopez laughs when asked about his weight loss, "The first thing one of the boys asked is - where's the rest of you? And you know, he had a point."

Over the last several months, Miguel Lopez was working out harder, more often and more diligently than he ever had in his life and the results are obvious. Said the Denver Broncos catcher, "I haven't literally lost a ton but I sure feel like I have."

It is widely expected that the 34 year old Lopez will see a lot better results on the field. Said one anonymous scout, "Miguel has always been an amazing athlete held back by his weight. With this change, I think he is in store to have a career year."
