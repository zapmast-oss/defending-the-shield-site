NBC Star of the Week Announced
Monday, April 15th , 2002
It was an extraordinary week for Jesus Torres of the Arizona Cardinals. First he goes on a hot hitting streak, batting .476, then he is voted the Player of the Week in the National Conference. Last week Torres went 10-for-21, racked up 2 home runs and 7 RBIs and scored 3 runs. It was a heck of a week for the Arizona first baseman.
