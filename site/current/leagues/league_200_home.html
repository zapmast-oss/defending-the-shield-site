Star CL Stuart Signs for 3 years
Sunday, May 5th , 2002
Fans hailed the signing of Sam Stuart today as the Dallas Cowboys closer came to terms with the club.

Set to make $4,140,000 over the upcoming 3 years, Stuart signed primarily out of loyalty to the organization, thrilling the most diehard supporters who think there isn't much loyalty in a market driven by free agency.

For the year, Stuart has 7 saves. In 12 relief appearances he has hurled 13 innings, fanned 12 and issued 1 walk with a 1-3 record. The Dallas reliever has recorded a 2.77 ERA and allowed the opposition to hit .288 when he is on the hill.
Tampa Bay Signs 2B Sandoval to 6-Year Extension
Sunday, May 5th , 2002
BNN reports that the Tampa Bay Buccaneers have just agreed with second baseman Aaron Sandoval on a contract extension.

The total value is $74,800,000 for 6 years, which equals $12,466,666 per season.

This year, Sandoval is hitting .318 with 5 home runs and 11 RBIs in 28 games played. He also boasts an OBP of .414 with 17 walks and 18 runs scored.
Quintana Has Season End
Sunday, May 5th , 2002
The Arizona Cardinals have lost the services of Roberto Quintana for the rest of this season. He suffered bone chips in his elbow in a recent ballgame and the follow-up report from the medical staff is not good. Quintana is out for the year.

This year, Quintana has earned 10 saves, made 14 relief appearances, fanned 12 hitters in 15 innings and posted an ERA of 2.40 and a 1-1 record.
