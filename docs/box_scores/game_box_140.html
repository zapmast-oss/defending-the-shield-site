Dolphins Get Past Oakland, 11-10
The Miami Dolphins weathered an outstanding game by Oakland third baseman Marcos Lemus to topple the Raiders, 11-10. Lemus was 2 for 5 with a home run. He drove in 4 runs and scored once. Miami reliever Brian Garrett was the winner. His record is now 1-0. Brandon McGee took the loss. The save was credited to Tony Palacios, his 3rd.

Yeow-whye Rackham was 1-4 with a double and a walk. He drove in 2 runs and scored once for Miami. The 23-year old catcher had a key at-bat in the top of the ninth. With two out and the bases loaded, he broke a 10-10 tie with a run-scoring walk off Brandon McGee. It was his 4th walk of the season.

"Crazy game," said Rackham after it ended.

At 11-6, the Dolphins have played well so far.
