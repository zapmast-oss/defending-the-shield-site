Betancur Stars as Browns Top Dolphins, 8-5
Browns right fielder Johnny Betancur put his stamp on his team's 8-5 win over the Miami Dolphins. Betancur went 3-3 with a triple, 2 singles, and 2 walks. Despite the loss, Miami is still 17-9.

Sean Hunt drove in one run for Cleveland. The 21-year old center fielder had a key at-bat in the top of the second. With one out and the bases loaded, he broke a 0-0 tie with a run-scoring sacrifice fly off Marco Rivera. It was his 2nd RBI of the season.

"Our approach was just to try to see something over the plate and be aggressive," Betancur told the Cleveland Times.
