Zamorano Leads Falcons to Win
The Atlanta Falcons and Minnesota Vikings lit up the scoreboard.

In a high-scoring contest between Atlanta and Minnesota, right fielder Sergio Zamorano was a difference-maker. Zamorano, who was drafted in the 2nd round of the 2002 draft, distinguished himself in the 10-8 Atlanta win. He went 3-4 with a home run, a double, and a walk. He scored 3 times and drove in 3. Chris Maria earned save number 3 this season.

In the top of the sixth, the Falcons grabbed the lead when Zamorano hit his 2nd home run of the season. The 3-run home run off Ricardo Legra made it 8-5, Atlanta.

"We're feeling good in this clubhouse right now after that one," Zamorano told the Atlanta Citizen.

Atlanta goes to 3-4.
